Privacy Policy

This policy covers the Resume Tailor web app, the static GitHub Pages version, and any Android or iOS app released under the same product name.

Effective date: March 20, 2026
This page is written to match the current product behavior: you provide resume and job materials, the app sends that content to the AI provider you choose, and the app returns tailored output. It is meant to satisfy the requirement for an app-specific privacy policy hosted at an active URL.

What We Collect

Information you provide

  • Resume text and files you upload or paste.
  • Job descriptions, job posting text, or job files you upload or paste.
  • API keys you enter for supported AI providers.
  • If you enable Notion features in supported builds, the content needed to log applications and generated outputs.

Technical data

  • Basic request and device data handled by the platforms and service providers you use, such as browser, operating system, and network metadata.
  • For local or mobile builds, files stay on your device unless you choose features that send them to an AI provider or to Notion.

How We Use Information

  • To read your resume and the target job description.
  • To generate a tailored resume and cover letter.
  • To create downloadable PDFs.
  • To save application history or generated output to Notion when you enable that feature.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the app.

How Information Is Shared

Resume Tailor does not sell your personal information.

  • AI providers: The app sends the text you provide to the AI provider you select, such as Anthropic or Google Gemini, so the provider can generate the tailored documents.
  • Notion: If you turn on Notion logging, the app sends the relevant job and output data to your Notion workspace.
  • Platform providers: Hosting, app-store, browser, operating-system, and infrastructure providers may process technical data needed to deliver the service.

Your use of third-party providers is also governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Where Processing Happens

The GitHub Pages build runs in the browser. In that version, your content and API key are sent directly from your device to the provider you choose. The project does not run a separate backend for that static page.

Local, Android, and iOS builds may process files on-device and may also transmit content to third-party services that you enable, including AI providers and Notion.

Data Retention

  • The static GitHub Pages build is designed so your content is processed in your browser and is not stored by a project-operated server.
  • Local builds store generated files on your device in the locations used by the app.
  • If you send data to an AI provider or to Notion, retention is subject to that provider's policies and your account settings with that provider.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to use Claude or Gemini.
  • You can choose whether to connect Notion.
  • You can remove local output files from your own device at any time.
  • You can stop using the app if you do not want your data sent to third-party providers.

Children

Resume Tailor is not directed to children. If you believe personal data from a child has been submitted through the app, contact us and we will review the request.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the product changes, when a new platform build is released, or when legal and store requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page shows the current version.
